About Us

The Indian Diplomat Summit Model United Nations (IDS-MUN) is a premier debating and diplomacy platform that brings together young minds to engage in global issues, negotiate solutions, and develop leadership skills. Organized by Yash Oswal, IDS MUN is committed to fostering critical thinking, international awareness, and policy-driven discussions among students. With a focus on realism and diplomatic excellence, IDS MUN simulates the workings of the United Nations, allowing delegates to represent nations, debate pressing global challenges, and collaborate on impactful resolutions. Whether you're a seasoned MUN participant or a first-time delegate, IDS MUN offers an enriching experience that sharpens your public speaking, negotiation, and problem-solving skills.
Join us at IDS MUN and be part of a new generation of changemakers shaping the future of global diplomacy!
